Our Animals

We currently raise registered Kinder goats, heritage cross pigs, rabbits, chickens, ducks, turkeys, and quail.

Our goal is to raise all of our own food, so our livestock serve multiple purposes. We get fresh milk from our goats and a variety of colorful eggs from our chickens! We strive to spend one on one time with all of our animals, so they get the best care possible.
