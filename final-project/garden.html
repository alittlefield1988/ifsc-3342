Our Garden

One of our main goals in pursuing sustainability is to grow all of our own food. Gardening plays a huge role in sustainability, and is financially a lot easier than raising livestock.

This year we purchased a greenhouse so we could start our plants off outside in the early spring. It has been a wonderful addition to our homestead!
